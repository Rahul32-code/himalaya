Welcome To Treasures of Himalaya

We are world reputeted travel agency

Welcome to Treasure of The Himalayas, where the majestic mountains meet the sacred heritage monasteries. For centuries, the Himalayas have been a treasure trove of natural beauty, spiritual wisdom, and cultural richness. Our journey began with a passion for exploration and deep reverence for the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the Himalayas. As travelers ourselves, we understand the importance of authentic experience, meaningful connection, and responsible travel practices.

At Treasure of the Himalayas, we’re dedicated to curating unforgettable journeys that allow you to immerse yourself in the breathtaking landscape, vibrant cultures, and spiritual traditions of the Himalayas. Right from trekking, hiking to monastery visits, and cultural festivals, pilgrimage tour,s and adventure sports, we’ll help you to discover the hidden treasure of this incredible region.

So, join us on a journey that will leave you with lifelong memories, a deeper appreciation for the Himalayas, and a sense of connection to the humble people and places that call this incredible journey home.

Our Mission

Treasure of the Himalayas is the premier travel company that specializes in curating unforgettable journeys to the majestic Himalayas. At present, there are so many online platforms to book hotels and vehicles etc. but they cut a huge amount of commission and give less to the hotels and vehicles by using their online platforms. Ultimately, our customers and travelers are being fooled and swindled huge money by these online platforms and pay high amounts. As an effect, this is also shutting down many physical and genuine travel companies globally. So, our mission to eradicate these mediator platforms and contact directly to us for better services in a reasonable price.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make people understand that travel to new places that increase their knowledge and rejuvenate them. Besides that, we want a layman to travel the world with less amount and keep incredible memories behind.

Why Choose Us

Experts Knowledge: Our team of experienced travel experts has in-depth knowledge of the Himalayas, allowing us to craft customized itineraries that cater to your unique preferences and interests. We advised our guests what to do and what not to do in the Himalayas and briefed every detail of the tour and itineraries.

Authentic experience: We believe in providing authentic experiences that allow our travelers to immerse themselves in the local culture, traditions, and natural beauty of the Himalayas. We advise our guests not to spit anywhere and take their garbage back and also not to pluck flowers to save our environment.

Sustainable Tourism: At Treasure of the Himalayas, we are committed to sustainable tourism practices that minimize our environmental impact and support local communities. Like: “Vocal for Local” theme. Likewise, we support and promote homestays in different areas. We also promote local handicrafts and handlooms and advice our guests to buy it as souvenirs. We encourage our guests to try local cuisine to promote and support small restaurants and fast food.

Personalized service: Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional customer service, ensuring every aspect of your journey is meticulously planned and accepted. We welcome every guest by offering khadaas (local garland) as a welcome gesture. Besides that, we also take special care of our guest when they have high altitude sickness and bring them down to a low altitude to save their lives.

Unparalleled Destinations: We offer a wide range of unparalleled international triangular destinations in the Himalayas including Nepal, Bhutan, and India. We offer customized tours and trekking packages catering to your unique preferences and interests.
